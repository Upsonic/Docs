At the early stage of GCA, builders wants to create an open-source alternative to OpenAI desktop app. After a while they got the point.

```
We have API of product, we have llms, tts and stt. Also we getting the power of all python AI tool ecosystem. 

So, lets create Intelligence for products !
```

then they built their product intelligence with GCA. 

**tldr; GCA is an Intelligence development framework, that able to connect, learn, explain and help about your product to end users.**
