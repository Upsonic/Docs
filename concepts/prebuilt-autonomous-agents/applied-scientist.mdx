An autonomous agent that runs inside Jupyter notebooks and validates whether a research paper's method delivers a real performance improvement over your existing model.

You give it two inputs — your current notebook and a research paper — and it takes it from there: studies both, runs the baseline, extracts metrics and scores, defines the benchmark criteria, applies the new method, and hands back a clear side-by-side comparison.
