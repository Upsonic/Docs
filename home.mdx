# About


The **Upsonic Platform** is your comprehensive solution for managing and optimizing your code and shared libraries. By integrating both client and server functionalities, Upsonic creates a seamless experience for making **dump and load operations**, organizing modules, and managing roles.

With the **Upsonic Client**, you can serialize and send your functions, classes, objects, or any variables to the **Upsonic Server**, effortlessly creating generative AI-supported libraries. This capability ensures speed and efficiency, allowing your team to focus on innovation without getting bogged down by spaghetti code and maintenance issues. Through **modern technologies** and a **permission-based infrastructure**, Upsonic provides easy storage, redesign, and delivery of shared libraries.

The **Upsonic Server** complements this by offering robust tools for organizing your modules and generating automatic documentation. It simplifies the process of creating, storing, and sharing libraries, enhancing team productivity. Plus, it supports multiple library versions within the same Python environment, eliminating the need for constant coordination and decision-making among team members.

<Note>
Upsonic does not require any licence for this features:
- Storing Functions
- Version Control
- AI Search
- AI Documentations, readmes, commit messages and release notes.

If you want to use other features like, Run History, Dependency Graph, LDAP Integration and more support you should get a [business edition]() licence.

</Note>

Our platform solves your toughest coding challenges, hides the complexity of **dependency management**, and delivers a user-friendly experience that empowers developers of all skill levels. Whether you're onboarding new team members or scaling up your operations, **Upsonic** is here to streamline your processes and boost your productivity.

Our **support team** is committed to your success. **Free tier users** can access assistance through **community channels**, while **business customers** receive dedicated support for their specific needs.

Experience the efficiency and innovation with the **Upsonic Platform**, your all-in-one solution for effortless code management and shared library optimization.

# Getting Support

### Getting Support

#### Community Edition
Community Edition users can access support through the following channels:

- **Log Bugs on GitHub**: Report any bugs on our [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Upsonic/Server/issues) page so they can be properly managed and addressed.

- **Flag Vulnerabilities**: If you discover a security vulnerability, please email us immediately at support@upsonic.com so we can address it promptly.

- **Document Issues**: Report any documentation issues via our [GitHub documentation channel](https://github.com/Upsonic/Docs/issues). You are also welcome to contribute directly to make our documentation better!

#### Business Edition Customers
For our Upsonic Business Edition customers, you can log tickets directly with our team:

- **Request Support**: [Email](support@upsonic.co) us to report a bug, ask a question, tell us about a documentation issue, or request a feature. Your tickets will be checked and resolved by our dedicated Upsonic staff within the agreed Service Level Agreement (**SLA**).

At Upsonic, we are dedicated to providing timely and effective support to ensure you have the best experience with our platform. Thank you for being a part of the Upsonic community!
