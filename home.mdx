# About


**Upsonic Community Edition (CE)** is our foundation. Its a comprehensive solution for managing and optimizing your code and shared libraries. You can store your functions inside of upsonic codebase and manage them via easy versioning. Read and share via integrated LLM Documentation pipeline. Distribute in your team with AI Search mechanism. 



**Upsonic Business Edition (BE)** is our commercial offering. With features geared towards businesses and larger organizations such as [LDAP Access Control](), [Run History](), [Dependency Graph](), and [dedicated support](), Upsonic BE is a powerful toolset that allows you to easily create and manage util libraries in Data Teams, Data Engineering Teams and Big Data Teams.




<Note>
Upsonic Business Edition requires a license key to install and use. If you don't currently have a license key, you can [purchase a license]().
</Note>


Our platform solves your toughest coding challenges, hides the complexity of dependency management, and delivers a user-friendly experience that empowers developers of all skill levels. Whether you're onboarding new team members or scaling up your operations, Upsonic is here to streamline your processes and boost your productivity.

Our **support team** is committed to your success. **Free tier users** can access assistance through **community channels**, while **business customers** receive dedicated support for their specific needs.

# Getting Support

### Getting Support

#### Community Edition, Storing, Docs and AI Features, Home & Student Users
Community Edition users can access support through the following channels:

- **Log Bugs on GitHub**: Report any bugs on our [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Upsonic/Server/issues) page so they can be properly managed and addressed.

- **Flag Vulnerabilities**: If you discover a security vulnerability, please email us immediately at support@upsonic.com so we can address it promptly.

- **Document Issues**: Report any documentation issues via our [GitHub documentation channel](https://github.com/Upsonic/Docs/issues). You are also welcome to contribute directly to make our documentation better!

#### Business Edition Customers
For our Upsonic Business Edition customers, you can log tickets directly with our team:

- **Request Support**: [Email](support@upsonic.co) us to report a bug, ask a question, tell us about a documentation issue, or request a feature. Your tickets will be checked and resolved by our dedicated Upsonic staff within the agreed Service Level Agreement (**SLA**).

At Upsonic, we are dedicated to providing timely and effective support to ensure you have the best experience with our platform. Thank you for being a part of the Upsonic community!
